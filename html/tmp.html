Get Involved

Product

Product Managers spearhead our client interactions and content strategy. In this cross-functional role, students develop their communication skills working with clients, designers and developers to ensure the final product meets all of our client's needs.

Development

In the fall semester, developers have the opportunity to learn web development skills and build a personal website. In the winter semester, these developers are paired with a team and put their skills to work on a client project.

Design

Designers get the chance to build their portfolio working with a small team and a real client. New designers also attend workshops to learn instustry skills and receive regular feedback on their work from experienced student designers.
